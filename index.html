Our mission at Caterkit is to make your life easier. As a seller, it can be difficult to keep on top of your inventory, sales and outgoings so we want to make things simpler.

Catering is not cheap.

You are no longer required to spend your profit on disposable catering supplies. Caterkit will provide you with your disposables as and when you need them.

So how does this work?

Caterkit is a platform and you are its users.

By requesting a free kit from us you are in turn providing us with a blank canvas, and it is on this canvas that the magic happens. When we recieve an order for a kit we work tirelessly to find you a suitable brand match. That is, a brand that we at Caterkit feel would appropriately match your target audience.

When we feel that we have discovered a suitable match we check in with you and talk things over. If you're happy with our choices then we continue with our process.

The next stage would be to hand over to our design team. Once we had established a brand that you would feel comfortable showcasing, it would time to design and create their campaign. Once the campaign has been approved, the branded disposables are shipped to you, ready for use. It's as simple as that.

Caterkit core value.

When we create a campaign we want to showcase the wonderful product that you are offering your customers as well as the brand we are representing.
